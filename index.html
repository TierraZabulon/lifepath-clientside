LifePath Number

How to get LifePath Number

  • Add the month and day of your birth date separately, then add the year together as a separate number.
  • Now, add together all the numbers of your birth year.
  • Next, add the number that was derived from your birth day and month to the number you landed on for your birth year.
  • Now add the two digits together to create one single number

  • Example:Birth Date: 7/22/1995
    7 = 7
    22 = 22
    1995 = 1 + 9 + 9 + 5 = 2 + 4 = 6
    7 + 22 + 6 = 35
    3 + 5 = 8
    Life Path Number: 8
